Proposal

High Concept

The game consists of 2 tanks that must try and shoot each other. The environment will be similar to that of a maze and the player will have to traverse the maze and shoot the other tank by ricocheting their bullets off the walls.

Genre

This would a kind of shooter game where you control a character that can shoot the enemy.

Platform

This will be a web-based game and you will need a mouse and keyboard.

Story

The year is 2150 and the US government has come up with a plan to entertain the masses. The idea is simple: 2 contestants enter the field of battle in state-of-the-art tanks. They must quickly examine the labyrinth around them and evaluate the situation. The prize is 1 billion dollars, but in order to achieve victory you must destroy your opponent. The government came up with some fancy name for it, but the public named it for it's fast action and explosive results: BALLISTIC.

Aesthetics

This game will have a white background and will consist of a maze of black walls. The two tank will be contrasting colors to differentiate them. The UI will be very simple and most likely in black to fit with the theme.

Gameplay

Controls

The tanks will be controlled with either WASD or the arrow keys (or both if you are playing multiplayer). Buttons will be clicked with the mouse.

Mechanics

Players will use the WASD or arrow keys to move their tanks. The vertical controls will move the tank forward and backwards while the horizontal controls will rotate the tank. There will be impassable walls scattered around the map similar to a maze. These walls will be formed randomly on level creation. The players can bounce bullets off the walls to reach places they couldn't before. 1 bullet will destroy the tank. The game will then end and you will be able to play again.
